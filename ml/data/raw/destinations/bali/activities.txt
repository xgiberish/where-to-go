Bali offers an enormous variety of activities for all travel styles. Adventure seekers can surf world-class breaks at Uluwatu and Padang Padang, white-water raft the Ayung River, or trek to the summit of Mount Batur for a stunning sunrise. Divers and snorkelers flock to Amed and Tulamben to explore vibrant coral reefs and the famous USAT Liberty shipwreck.

For cultural immersion, visitors can attend traditional Kecak fire dances at Tanah Lot, take offerings at sunrise, or study cooking, silver crafting, or traditional Balinese painting in Ubud. Wellness travelers can choose from hundreds of spas offering traditional massages, flower baths, and multi-day yoga and meditation retreats.
