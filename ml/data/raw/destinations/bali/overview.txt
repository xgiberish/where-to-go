Bali is an Indonesian island known for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches, and coral reefs. The island is home to religious sites such as cliffside Uluwatu Temple. To the south, the beach city of Kuta has lively bars, while Seminyak, Sanur, and Nusa Dua are popular resort towns. Bali has a tropical climate with warm temperatures year-round, making it an ideal destination for travelers seeking sun, culture, and natural beauty.

The best time to visit is during the dry season from April to October. The island draws millions of visitors each year who come for surfing, yoga retreats, spiritual healing, and the renowned Balinese hospitality.
